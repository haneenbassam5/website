History Of Store

Neon, marketed as NEON until 2020, is a subscription video on demand service offering a range of television shows and movies. The platform is a division of Sky Network Television Limited.

In 11 June, Neon experienced technical difficulties that caused users to be locked out of the app.[13] In mid-June 2020, Sky announced that Lightbox would be merged into Neon, with Lightbox app being replaced by a Neon app on 7 July 2020. The merged service retained the Neon brand but continues to use Lightbox's interface and incorporates content drawn from both Neon and the old Lightbox.
